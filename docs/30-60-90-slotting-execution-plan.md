# 30-60-90 Day Slotting & Space Management Execution Plan

## Overview
This execution plan outlines a structured, data-driven approach to warehouse slotting and space optimization aligned to operational efficiency, safety, and scalability.

---

## 0–30 Days | Stabilize & Diagnose
**Objective:** Reduce immediate operational risk and establish visibility.

### Key Actions
- Validate ABC classification against SKU velocity
- Identify over-capacity pick faces
- Isolate Hazmat SKUs into compliant reserve locations
- Flag high-congestion zones
- Establish baseline KPIs

### KPIs
- % SKUs over capacity
- Hazmat compliance rate
- % volume flowing through Fast zones

---

## 31–60 Days | Optimize & Execute
**Objective:** Improve flow efficiency and rebalance space.

### Key Actions
- Re-slot A-items into golden pick zones
- Push C-items to deeper reserve storage
- Right-size pick-face cube using utilization thresholds
- Introduce seasonal vs core inventory logic
- Pilot changes in high-impact zones

### KPIs
- Reduction in over-capacity SKUs
- Pick-face utilization variance
- Congestion risk reduction

---

## 61–90 Days | Standardize & Govern
**Objective:** Make improvements repeatable and scalable.

### Key Actions
- Finalize slotting standards by ABC class
- Establish monthly slotting review cadence
- Integrate KPIs into operational reviews
- Align slotting logic with WMS configuration
- Document slotting governance playbooks

### KPIs
- Sustained capacity compliance
- Slotting cycle time
- Hazmat safety adherence

---

## Business Impact
- Improved picker efficiency
- Reduced congestion and safety risk
- Scalable slotting framework aligned to growth
